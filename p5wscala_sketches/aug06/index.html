Keyboard controls

q/w - Switch between wireframe/mesh drawing
a/s - Switch between spherical/planar/torodial rendering
z/x - Switch between white/starry background
n - generate random world
g - toggle axes (only visible in planar/torodial mode)

Mouse controls

left click+drag to rotate
mouse-wheel or right click+drag to zoom
doubleclick to reset camera
