Keyboard controls

'b' - toggle blur
'p' - toggle posterize
space - pause
'r' - reset
'q' - next group
'g' - toggle greater than/less than

What's goin' on eh?

There are things called "Runner"s that move in straight lines, leaving some sort of color behind them until they hit either the edge of the applet, or they hit a color that is within a color distance threshold to their own color. When that happens, the Runner chooses a random direction and tries again. If that fails, the Runner teleports to a random location on the screen and tries again. If THAT fails 100 times, the Runner does nothing until the next frame.

You may choose to apply a blur and/or posterize filter on each frame; interesting figures and combinations arise from their interplay with Runners. "Next group" toggles through different groups of runners (black only, black/white, 5 HSB runners).

The gt/lt toggle defines whether Runners can only move through colors that are greater than/less than the color threshold. e.g. If the distance threshold is .80, then a black Runner can only move on colors that are:
     More than .8 distance from black for "gt".
     Less than .8 distance from black for "lt".
In general, "gt" means Runners will hit their own color, and "lt" means Runners will hit other colors.
